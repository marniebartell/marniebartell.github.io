About me

Hello! I'm Marnie...

I am a user experience (UX) advisor with over 25 years of experience creating, managing, and leading web design, strategy, and front-end development.

My greatest strength is creative problem-solving and I have a talent for seeing possibilities and challenging the status quo.

I am passionate about making technology easier for people to use, and I value figuring things out and getting things done.

I am known for combining technology and design to help people get the information they need. I spent the early years of my career helping people get onto the Internet and the last 20 years helping people get health information.

I have worked with notable clients including various Alberta Health Services teams, Alberta Health, Health Link Alberta, Inform Alberta, OpenText, Microsoft, and Disney.

I love camping, music, cross-country skiing, cycling, hiking, and being outside. I enjoy drawing, doodling, and journaling as a creative outlet (and to relax).

I also love spending time with my family, including my husband, twins sons, and two dachshunds.
